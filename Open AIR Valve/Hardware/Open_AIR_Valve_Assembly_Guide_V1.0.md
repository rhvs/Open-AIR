# Important Assembly Notes

‚ö†Ô∏è **Use hand tools only. Do NOT use electric screwdrivers.**

The bolts thread directly into plastic. Over-tightening can strip the plastic threads.

This design intentionally avoids metal inserts to improve recyclability at end of life.

Tighten bolts firmly, but only hand-tight.
